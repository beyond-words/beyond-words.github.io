Beyond Words Speech-Language Consultants is a team of four Speech-Language Pathologists providing private services for individuals of all ages in Regina and southern Saskatchewan. Each of our therapists brings different experiences and areas of expertise to the group, and we are pleased to offer services for individuals with a variety of communication needs.


We will help you develop optimal speech and language skills to communicate to your full potential. We are committed to implementing exceptional, family-centered, therapy services to meet your individual needs! We recognize each client is unique, and value partnering with our clients to develop individualized therapy programs.


Contact us today to discuss your concerns!
